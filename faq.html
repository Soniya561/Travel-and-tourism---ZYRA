Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about booking, payments, changes and more.

How do I book a package?

Use the Book Now button to select destination, dates and travelers. Review the summary and proceed to checkout.

Can I reserve now and pay later?

For most packages we support a small advance payment to block seats. The balance is due before ticketing. Availability may vary by package.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major cards, UPI, and net banking. On checkout, choose your preferred method.

Is my payment secure?

Yes. Payments are processed over 256‑bit SSL. We do not store sensitive card data on our servers.

My card was charged but I didn’t get confirmation.

Please wait a few minutes and check your spam folder. If still missing, contact support with your transaction reference.

Can I change travel dates after booking?

Date changes are subject to airline/hotel rules and fare difference. Contact us at least 72 hours before departure.

What is your cancellation policy?

Refer to our cancellation policy for package-specific timelines and fees.

Do you assist with visas?

Yes, we provide guidance and documentation support for many destinations. Visa approval is at the discretion of the issuing authority.

What documents are required for international travel?

A valid passport, visa (if applicable), and health documents per destination rules. Always verify the latest requirements.

Can you customize itineraries?

Absolutely. Share your preferences and we’ll tailor a private itinerary including activities, transfers, and stays.

Do you offer travel insurance?

We can include comprehensive travel insurance from our partners upon request. Coverage varies by destination and age.
